The Bishop of Vasai

Serving the spiritual needs of the community.

Most Rev. Thomas Francis D'Souza

Appointed by Pope Francis on November 9, 2024 | Episcopal Ordination: December 15, 2024

Early Life and Priesthood

Born on March 23, 1970, in Chulne, Vasai diocese. He studied philosophy and theology at Saint Pius X College in Goregaon and holds a Master's degree in Philosophy and Religion from Madras University. He was ordained a priest on April 18, 1998, for the Diocese of Vasai.

Previous Roles

Before his appointment as Bishop, Most Rev. Thomas D'Souza served in various capacities within the diocese:

  • Deputy Parish Priest of Holy Spirit in Nandakhal (1998-2002)
  • Rector of the Minor Seminary in Remedy (2000-2001, 2005-2007)
  • Parish Priest of Divine Mercy in Bhiwandi (2003-2005)
  • Parish Priest of Saint James in Agashi (2007-2013)
  • Parish Priest of Holy Spirit and Head of Saint Joseph High School and Junior College in Nandakhal (2019-present)

Episcopal Motto and Vision

Bishop D'Souza has expressed his deep commitment to focusing on the welfare of the poor, marginalized, and farming communities, as well as the preservation of the local ecology. His pastoral approach emphasizes social justice, environmental stewardship, and inclusive community development.

Legacy of Leadership

Honoring the spiritual shepherds who have guided the Diocese of Vasai through decades of faith and service

Most Rev. Thomas Dabre

May 22, 1998 – April 4, 2009

First Bishop of Vasai, established the foundation of the diocese and led the community through its formative years.

Most Rev. Felix Anthony Machado

November 10, 2009 – June 6, 2024

Strengthened the diocese through significant growth and modernization, fostering community development and spiritual renewal.

Most Rev. Thomas Francis D'Souza

December 15, 2024 – Present

Current Bishop, bringing fresh vision for environmental stewardship and social justice to guide the diocese into the future.
