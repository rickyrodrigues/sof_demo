Our Journey of Faith

Discover the story, mission, and people behind our vibrant Catholic community

Our History

1980s

Foundation Years

Our community was born from the vision of devout individuals who gathered for prayer and fellowship, laying the groundwork for what would become a beacon of hope in Vasai.

1991

Josodi Cross Centenary

We organized the historic centenary celebration of the Josodi Cross, marking 100 years of this sacred landmark erected in 1891. This event strengthened our community bonds and connected us to our Catholic heritage.

2020

Digital Faith During Pandemic

Adapted to virtual prayer sessions and online fellowship, proving that faith transcends physical boundaries and community can thrive even in challenging times.

Today

Growing Community

Continuing to flourish with expanded ministries, youth programs, and community service initiatives that bring people together across all ages in faith and fellowship.

Our Mission

To foster a dynamic Catholic faith, rooted in prayer and service, inspiring individuals to live lives of love and witness to Christ in the Vasai Diocese and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and faith in Vasai, inspiring lives through deep spiritual formation, vibrant community engagement, and compassionate service.
